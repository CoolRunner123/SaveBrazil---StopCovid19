"When people are crying out for help, we rise to make a difference...even if its thousands of miles away"

Through this website, we seeked to analyze deforestation and particulate matter concentrations in Brazil and the connection it has to increasing infection rates of Covid-19 throughout Brazil. Because of the deforestation of the Amazon rainforest and differences in socie-economic status between individuals who live in cities and individuals who live in the Amazons, Covid-19 is hitting the indeginous people living in the Amazon rainforest area much harder. We hope that this website would spread awareness about this issue and encourage people to help out in any way they can.

Moving forward, we would like to improve out website by collecting more data regarding deforestation in the Amazons and particulate matter concentrations.
